Dhaka: The BJP’s electoral tsunami in Uttar Pradesh will not just dramatically change the party’s numbers in the assembly, but will have an immediate impact on the next Presidential elections in July when President Pranab Mukherjee completes his term.For National Democratic Alliance or NDA, the path to having its nominee elected as the next President has become much easier after the BJP and its allies won 325 lawmakers in UP, 57 in Uttarakhand and impressive 21 in Manipur. Since the results, the BJP has announced it is all set to take charge in the state, claiming it has support of 31 legislators.
