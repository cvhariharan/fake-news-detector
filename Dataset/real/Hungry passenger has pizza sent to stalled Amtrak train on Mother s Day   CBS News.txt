NEW YORK -- A pizza deliveryman came to the rescue of passengers stranded for hours on an Amtrak train.Passengers got hungry when the New York-to-Washington, D.C. train experienced mechanical issues and got stuck in Delaware around 7 p.m. Sunday, CBS New York reports.One employee gave away chocolate and some moms shared their Mother's Day champagne gifts.But one passenger thought outside the box and ordered a pizza from a local restaurant.Video shows the dedicated deliveryman walking along the tracks near a creek to find the customer and deliver the pies.The train was delayed nearly three hours.
