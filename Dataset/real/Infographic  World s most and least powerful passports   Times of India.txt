It’s a tiny island, but Singapore now has the most powerful passport in the world with its citizens welcome without a visa or where they can get a visa on arrival in 159 countries. This is also the first time that an Asian country has bagged the top rank. India’s ranking has improved from 78 in 2016 to 75 in 2017. Interestingly, USA’s visa free score has dropped with Turkey and the Central African Republic the most recent countries to revoke visa-free status to US.
