SRINAGAR: Three militants belonging to the Jaish-e-Mohammad have been arrested in central Kashmir's Budgam district, police said here on Sunday. In a major success, three militants were arrested in Budgam and an SLR rifle, a pistol and few grenades were seized from their possession, a police official said. The investigation is going on, the official said, adding that they belong to a Jaish-e-Mohammad outfit. He did not share further details.
