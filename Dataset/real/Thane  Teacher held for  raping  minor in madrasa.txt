A five-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a teacher inside a madrasa in Mumbra area near here, following which the accused was arrested, the police said on Saturday.“The 35-year-old accused, who hailed from Bihar, raped the girl a number of times last week. He was arrested last night,” C M Mujwar, Inspector at Mumbra police station, said. He said the accused was an Arabic language teacher who had joined the madrasa 15 days ago.
